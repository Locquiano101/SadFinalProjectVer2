About PENRO


The Camarines Norte PENRO plays a pivotal role in supporting the province's sustainable development by preserving and protecting ecosystems that are vital to key sectors such as agriculture, fisheries, and tourism. As a steward of the province’s natural resources, PENRO is committed to safeguarding biodiversity, promoting environmental sustainability, and ensuring that natural resources are responsibly utilized for the benefit of current and future generations.


PENRO actively engages with a diverse range of stakeholders, including Local Government Units (LGUs), Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs), community groups, and the private sector. This collaboration fosters a holistic approach to environmental preservation, ensuring that policies and programs are inclusive and community-driven. By working closely with these stakeholders, PENRO helps align provincial environmental goals with national policies, enabling more effective implementation of conservation and resource management initiatives.


Through its various programs, services, and partnerships, PENRO remains dedicated to the protection and sustainable management of Camarines Norte’s natural resources. The office not only addresses immediate environmental concerns but also works towards long-term solutions that foster ecological balance, ensuring that the province’s natural wealth can be enjoyed by generations to come.


FOREST TREE SEEDLINGS REQUEST PROCESS

HEAD OF THE OFFICE: ENGR. LEOPOLDO P. BADIOLA


Office/Division: PROVINCIAL ENVIRONMENT AND NATURAL RESOURCES OFFICE


Type Of Transaction: PROCESSING OF REQUEST FOR FOREST TREE SEEDLINGS


Who May Avail: ALL INTERESTED INDIVIDUALS

Follow-Up Request At PG-PENRO Provides Client With A Short Briefing On The Service; Study The Request And Determine The Number Of Seedlings To Be Given; And Provide Requirement (Promissory Note) To The Client 30 Minutes Engr. Fill-Up Promissory Note Provided By PENRO Recommend To The Governor The Number Of Seedlings To Be Given 15 Minutes Engr. Leopoldo P. Badiola
Office Of The Governor Approve/Disapprove Recommendation Of PENRO And Forwards The Promissory Note To PENRO Depending On The Availability Of The Signatory Office Of The Governor/Authorized Representative
Proceed To PG-PENRO To Follow-Up The Promissory Note. If Approved, Receive A Copy Of The Document. Release A Copy Of The Promissory Note To The Requesting Individual 10 Minutes Ms. Emerine A. San Gaspar
